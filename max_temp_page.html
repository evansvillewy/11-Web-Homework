Max Temperature

As latitude increases, the temperature falls. As latitude decreases, temperature rises. Generally, it gets warmer at the equator and colder at the poles. Latitude is a measure of distance a location is away from the equator. The further away from the equator a location is, the less sunlight/heat it gets. This is due to the round shape of the earth. In addition, due to the tilt of the earth on its axis, the sun's rays hit the earth at a right angle at the equator, intensifying it's impact. At the poles, this impact is dimished significantly causing heat loss.
